# Skill: LinkedIn Poster
## Purpose
Automatically generates and posts business content to LinkedIn to drive engagement and sales.

## Instructions
When invoked, you should:
1. **Analyze Recent Activity**: Look at `Dashboard.md` and `Done` folder for notable achievements this week.
2. **Draft Content**: Create 3 variations of a professional LinkedIn post (e.g., informative, achievement-based, or inquiry-based).
3. **Approval Required**: Save the drafts to `Needs_Action/LINKEDIN_POST_DRAFT.md`.
4. **Execution**: Once approved (moved to `Approved/`), use the LinkedIn integration script to post the content.

## Parameters
- `topic`: Specific topic to post about.
- `tone`: (Optional) Professional, Excited, or Thought-Provoking.

## Output Format
A confirmation of the draft creation and a request for review.
